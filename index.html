The Power of Movement: How Our Technology Works

Our device utilizes advanced Relaxor Piezoelectric Single Crystal Multilayer Stacks (RPSEHT) that convert mechanical energy from your movements into electrical energy. By tuning the resonant frequency, we've optimized the system to harvest maximum energy from everyday activities like walking, jogging, and biking.
